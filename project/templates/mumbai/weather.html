What to Wear

When visiting Mumbai, it's essential to dress for comfort due to the city's humid and warm climate. During the summer, light, breathable fabrics like cotton are highly recommended to stay cool. If you're traveling during the monsoon, an umbrella and waterproof clothing will be useful as rain showers are common. In the cooler months, you can enjoy wearing comfortable, light layers. Remember, modesty is appreciated in some areas, so covering your shoulders and knees is advisable, especially when visiting temples or religious sites.

Weather Overview

Mumbai is known for its tropical climate, with hot and humid conditions throughout most of the year. The summer months (March to June) can be particularly hot, with temperatures often rising above 35°C (95°F). The monsoon season (June to September) brings heavy rains, while the cooler months from October to February offer more pleasant weather, with temperatures ranging from 25°C to 30°C (77°F to 86°F).

Famous Food Spots in Cape Town

Street Food

Cape Town's vibrant street food scene is a reflection of its diverse culture. Visit markets like the **Old Biscuit Mill** and **Hout Bay Market** to savor dishes such as bunny chow, gourmet boerewors rolls, and Cape Malay samosas. Don’t miss out on the local favorite: Gatsby sandwiches, a massive, flavor-packed sub filled with chips, meats, and sauces.

Fine Dining

Cape Town boasts a world-class fine dining scene, with restaurants offering breathtaking views and award-winning menus. Indulge in seasonal dishes at **La Colombe**, consistently ranked among the best in the world, or enjoy a sustainable, seafood-focused meal at **The Test Kitchen**. For a memorable experience, try **Signal Restaurant** at the Cape Grace Hotel, blending Cape flavors with global inspirations.

Desserts

Cape Town is a paradise for dessert lovers. Treat yourself to freshly baked koeksisters, a traditional South African sweet, or malva pudding, a moist sponge cake served with custard. Artisan bakeries like **Charly’s Bakery** offer colorful cakes and cupcakes, while **Unframed Ice Cream** serves up unique flavors crafted from local ingredients. Don’t forget to try Cape Town’s renowned Rooibos tea-infused desserts.
